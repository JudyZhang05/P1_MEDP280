How To Proceed

  • You can use the left and right arrow keys to read, or on mobile you can tap the left or right side of the screen.
  • Tap the top right sound icon to play music or press "m" on your keyboard to toggle.
  • On some drawings, there are second parts to them that you can view by tapping the image or pressing space on your keyboard.
  • That's it. Enjoy!
